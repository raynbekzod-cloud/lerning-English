🎯 Modal Fe'llar / Modal Verbs

Qobiliyat, ruxsat, majburiyat, maslahat va ehtimollik ifodalari

✅ CAN / COULD

  • can - qobiliyat, ruxsat (I can swim)
  • can't - qobiliyatsizlik (I can't dance)
  • could - o'tgan zamondagi qobiliyat (I could run fast)
  • could - muloyim iltimos (Could you help me?)

📌 MUST / SHOULD

  • must - majburiyat (You must stop)
  • mustn't - qat'iy taqiq (You mustn't smoke here)
  • should - maslahat (You should see a doctor)
  • shouldn't - salbiy maslahat (You shouldn't eat too much)

🔄 MAY / MIGHT

  • may - ehtimol (It may rain)
  • might - kam ehtimol (I might come late)
  • may - ruxsat so'rash (May I come in?)

📖 Misollar:

✅ She can speak three languages.

✅ Could you please open the door?

✅ You must wear a seatbelt.

✅ You should exercise more.

✅ It may snow tomorrow.

✅ I might go to the party.

🎭 Active & Passive Voice / Aktiv va Passiv

🎯 Active

Subject + Verb + Object

  • ✅ I write a letter.
  • ✅ She cooks dinner.

🔄 Passive

Object + be + V3 + by Subject

  • ✅ A letter is written by me.
  • ✅ Dinner is cooked by her.

📝 10 ta test

3. Active: They will build a house. → Passive: A house _____ built.

4. Active: He is repairing the car. → Passive: The car _____ repaired.

5. Active: They have finished the work. → Passive: The work _____ finished.

6. Active: He helps me. → Passive: I _____ helped by him.

7. Active: She loved him. → Passive: He _____ loved by her.

🗣️ Reported Speech / Ko'chirma gap

📌 Qoidalar

  • Present Simple → Past Simple
  • Present Continuous → Past Continuous
  • will → would
  • can → could
  • now → then, today → that day

📌 Misollar

  • ✅ "I work hard" → He said he worked hard.
  • ✅ "I am reading" → She said she was reading.
  • ✅ "I will come" → He said he would come.
