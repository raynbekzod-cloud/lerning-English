🎯 Modal Fe'llar

✅ CAN / COULD

  • can - qobiliyat, ruxsat (I can swim)
  • could - o'tgan qobiliyat (I could run fast)
  • could - muloyim iltimos (Could you help?)

📌 MUST / SHOULD

  • must - majburiyat (You must stop)
  • mustn't - qat'iy taqiq (You mustn't smoke)
  • should - maslahat (You should see a doctor)

🔄 MAY / MIGHT

  • may - ehtimol (It may rain)
  • might - kam ehtimol (I might come late)
  • may - ruxsat so'rash (May I come in?)
