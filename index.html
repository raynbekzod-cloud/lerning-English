📚 12 ingliz zamoni / 12 English Tenses

Har bir zamon o'z rangida | 5+ misol | Testlar

🔵

Present Simple

I/You/We/They + V1 | He/She/It + V1 + s

📌 5+ misol:

✅ I work every day.

✅ She works in an office.

✅ They play football on Sundays.

✅ He goes to school by bus.

✅ We eat breakfast at 8 AM.

✅ The sun rises in the east.

always usually often sometimes never every day on Mondays

Present Continuous

I + am + Ving | He/She/It + is + Ving | You/We/They + are + Ving

📌 5+ misol:

✅ I am reading now.

✅ She is cooking dinner.

✅ They are playing outside.

✅ He is sleeping at the moment.

✅ We are watching TV.

✅ The baby is crying.

now right now at the moment today look! listen!

🟡

Present Perfect

I/You/We/They + have + V3 | He/She/It + has + V3

📌 5+ misol:

✅ She has gone to London.

✅ They have seen that movie.

✅ He has eaten already.

📖 In / On / At - Predloglar

🕐 IN

  • in January, in July
  • in 2020, in 1995
  • in the morning, in the evening
  • in summer, in winter
  • in Uzbekistan, in Tashkent

📅 ON

  • on Monday, on Friday
  • on 15th June, on May 1st
  • on my birthday
  • on the table, on the wall
  • on the phone, on TV

⏰ AT

  • at 5 PM, at 8:30
  • at Christmas, at Easter
  • at lunchtime
  • at night
  • at the bus stop

📖 Ingliz tilidagi barcha predloglar / All Prepositions

📍 ABOVE

📌 ustida, yuqorida

✅ The sun is above the clouds.

✅ Hang the picture above the sofa.

📍 AFTER

📌 keyin

✅ We'll eat after the movie.

✅ He came after me.

📍 ALONG

📌 bo'ylab

✅ Walk along the river.

✅ Trees grow along the road.

📍 AROUND

📌 atrofida

✅ He looked around the room.

📍 AT

📌 da (joy/vaqt)

✅ The class starts at 9 AM.

📍 BEFORE

📌 oldin

✅ He arrived before me.

📍 BEHIND

📝 Barcha predloglar bo'yicha 8 ta test / 8 Tests on All Prepositions

3. We'll eat _____ the movie. (before / after / during)

4. The cat is _____ the table. (under / above / on)

5. I'm going _____ school. (to / at / for)

6. He left _____ saying goodbye. (without / with / for)

7. I waited _____ an hour. (for / during / since)

8. The book was written _____ him. (by / from / of)
