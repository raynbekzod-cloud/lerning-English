📖 In / On / At - Predloglar (Joy va Vaqt)

📍 IN (JOY & VAQT)

  • ✅ in Tashkent, in a room
  • ✅ in January, in summer, in 2025
  • ✅ in the morning / afternoon
  • ✅ in 5 minutes, in a week

📍 ON (JOY & VAQT)

  • ✅ on the table, on the wall
  • ✅ on Monday, on Friday morning
  • ✅ on 15th June, on my birthday
  • ✅ on holiday, on time, on TV

📍 AT (JOY & VAQT)

  • ✅ at the bus stop, at home
  • ✅ at 5 PM, at 8:30
  • ✅ at night, at noon, at midnight
  • ✅ at the weekend, at Christmas

📝 Articles / Artikllar (1-dars)

A, An, The - qo'llanish qoidalari

🔵 A

Undosh tovushdan oldin

  • ✅ a book, a car, a house
  • ✅ a university (ju:)
  • ✅ a dog, a cat, a teacher

🟢 AN

Unli tovushdan oldin (a,e,i,o,u)

  • ✅ an apple, an egg, an ice-cream
  • ✅ an hour (aunli 'h')
  • ✅ an honest man, an umbrella

🟡 THE

Aniq, ma'lum narsa

  • ✅ the sun, the moon, the sky
  • ✅ the best, the first, the same
  • ✅ the door (o'sha eshik)

📊 Degrees of Comparison / Sifat darajalari

Sifatlarning 3 darajasi: Oddiy, Qiyosiy, Eng yuqori

1️⃣ Positive Degree

Oddiy daraja

  • fast (tez), big (katta), beautiful (chiroyli)

2️⃣ Comparative Degree

Qiyosiy daraja (ikki narsa)

  • faster than, bigger than, more beautiful than

3️⃣ Superlative Degree

Eng yuqori daraja

  • the fastest, the biggest, the most beautiful

📝 5+ misol:

  • ✅ My car is faster than yours.
  • ✅ She is more beautiful than her sister.
  • ✅ This is the most expensive phone.
  • ✅ He runs faster than me.
  • ✅ Today is hotter than yesterday.
  • ✅ This is the easiest test.

🎭 Active & Passive Voice / Aktiv va Passiv

Aktiv: ish-harakatni bajaruvchi muhim | Passiv: natija muhim

🎯 Active Voice (Aktiv)

Subject + Verb + Object

  • ✅ I write a letter.
  • ✅ She cooks dinner.
  • ✅ The cat ate the mouse.

🔄 Passive Voice (Passiv)

Object + be + V3 + by + Subject

  • ✅ A letter is written by me.
  • ✅ Dinner is cooked by her.
  • ✅ The mouse was eaten by the cat.
ZamonActivePassive
Present SimpleI writeIt is written
Past SimpleI wroteIt was written
Future SimpleI will writeIt will be written
Present ContinuousI am writingIt is being written
Present PerfectI have writtenIt has been written

📝 5+ misol:

  • ✅ Active: She writes a letter. → Passive: A letter is written by her.
  • ✅ Active: He ate an apple. → Passive: An apple was eaten by him.
  • ✅ Active: They will build a house. → Passive: A house will be built.
  • ✅ Active: I am repairing the car. → Passive: The car is being repaired.
  • ✅ Active: She has finished the work. → Passive: The work has been finished.
