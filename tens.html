English Tenses (12)

Formula + Qachon ishlatilishi + Misollar + Testlar

Tutorial

<

Present Simple

Formula: I/You/We/They + V1  |  He/She/It + V1+s
Qachon ishlatiladi: Har kungi odatlar, umumiy haqiqatlar, doimiy holatlar

I work every day.

She works in an office.

The sun rises in the east.

Present Continuous

Formula: am/is/are + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: Ayni hozir sodir bo'layotgan ish-harakatlar

I am reading now.

She is cooking dinner.

The baby is crying.

Present Perfect

Formula: have/has + V3
Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tgan va hozirgi vaqt orasidagi bog'liqlik, natija muhim

I have finished my homework.

She has gone to London.

I have never been to Paris.

Present Perfect Cont.

Formula: have/has + been + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda boshlanib hozirgacha davom etayotgan ish

I have been studying for 3 hours.

She has been working since morning.

It has been raining all day.

Past Simple

Formula: V2 (ed yoki 2-shakl)
Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda tugagan ish-harakatlar

I worked yesterday.

She went to London.

I bought a new car.

Past Continuous

Formula: was/were + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda ma'lum vaqtda davom etgan ish

I was sleeping at 10 PM.

They were playing football.

It was raining heavily.

Past Perfect

Formula: had + V3
Qachon ishlatiladi: Bir o'tmish ishi boshqasidan oldin sodir bo'lgan

I had eaten before he came.

She had left by then.

The train had left.

Past Perfect Cont.

Formula: had been + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda bir ishdan oldin davom etgan ish

I had been waiting for an hour.

She had been studying all day.

It had been raining all night.

Future Simple

Formula: will + V1
Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda sodir bo'ladigan ish-harakatlar

I will call you tomorrow.

She will come to the party.

It will rain today.

Future Continuous

Formula: will be + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtda davom etadigan ish

I will be working at 5 PM.

They will be traveling tomorrow.

The sun will be shining.

Future Perfect

Formula: will have + V3
Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtgacha tugaydigan ish

I will have finished by 6 PM.

She will have left by then.

The movie will have started.

Future Perfect Cont.

Formula: will have been + Ving
Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtgacha davom etgan ish

I will have been working for 5 years.

She will have been living here since 2020.

They will have been studying for 3 hours.
