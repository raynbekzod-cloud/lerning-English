🎭 Active & Passive Voice

🎯 Active

Subject + Verb + Object

  • I write a letter.

🔄 Passive

Object + be + V3 + by Subject

  • A letter is written by me.

📝 Testlar

1. I write a book. → A book _____ by me.

2. She ate an apple. → An apple _____ by her.

3. They will build a house. → A house _____ built.

4. He helps me. → I _____ helped by him.

5. She loved him. → He _____ loved by her.
