📚 12 ingliz zamoni

Formula + Qachon ishlatilishi + Misollar + Testlar

🔵

Present Simple

📌 Formula: I/You/We/They + V1  |  He/She/It + V1+s
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Har kungi odatlar, umumiy haqiqatlar, doimiy holatlar

✅ I work every day.

✅ She works in an office.

✅ The sun rises in the east.

📝 Testlar

🟢

Present Continuous

📌 Formula: am/is/are + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Ayni hozir sodir bo'layotgan ish-harakatlar

✅ I am reading now.

✅ She is cooking dinner.

✅ The baby is crying.

📝 Testlar

🟡

Present Perfect

📌 Formula: have/has + V3
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tgan va hozirgi vaqt orasidagi bog'liqlik, natija muhim

✅ I have finished my homework.

✅ She has gone to London.

✅ I have never been to Paris.

📝 Testlar

🟠

Present Perfect Cont.

📌 Formula: have/has + been + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda boshlanib hozirgacha davom etayotgan ish

✅ I have been studying for 3 hours.

✅ She has been working since morning.

✅ It has been raining all day.

📝 Testlar

⚪

Past Simple

📌 Formula: V2 (ed yoki 2-shakl)
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda tugagan ish-harakatlar

✅ I worked yesterday.

✅ She went to London.

✅ I bought a new car.

📝 Testlar

🟣

Past Continuous

📌 Formula: was/were + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda ma'lum vaqtda davom etgan ish

✅ I was sleeping at 10 PM.

✅ They were playing football.

✅ It was raining heavily.

📝 Testlar

🤎

Past Perfect

📌 Formula: had + V3
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Bir o'tmish ishi boshqasidan oldin sodir bo'lgan

✅ I had eaten before he came.

✅ She had left by then.

✅ The train had left.

📝 Testlar

🧡

Past Perfect Cont.

📌 Formula: had been + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: O'tmishda bir ishdan oldin davom etgan ish

✅ I had been waiting for an hour.

✅ She had been studying all day.

✅ It had been raining all night.

📝 Testlar

🔮

Future Simple

📌 Formula: will + V1
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda sodir bo'ladigan ish-harakatlar

✅ I will call you tomorrow.

✅ She will come to the party.

✅ It will rain today.

📝 Testlar

💚

Future Continuous

📌 Formula: will be + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtda davom etadigan ish

✅ I will be working at 5 PM.

✅ They will be traveling tomorrow.

✅ The sun will be shining.

📝 Testlar

💜

Future Perfect

📌 Formula: will have + V3
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtgacha tugaydigan ish

✅ I will have finished by 6 PM.

✅ She will have left by then.

✅ The movie will have started.

📝 Testlar

🧡

Future Perfect Cont.

📌 Formula: will have been + Ving
📖 Qachon ishlatiladi: Kelajakda ma'lum vaqtgacha davom etgan ish

✅ I will have been working for 5 years.

✅ She will have been living here since 2020.

✅ They will have been studying for 3 hours.

🎯 Modal Fe'llar

✅ CAN / COULD

  • can - qobiliyat, ruxsat (I can swim)
  • could - o'tgan qobiliyat (I could run fast)
  • could - muloyim iltimos (Could you help?)

📌 MUST / SHOULD

  • must - majburiyat (You must stop)
  • mustn't - qat'iy taqiq (You mustn't smoke)
  • should - maslahat (You should see a doctor)

🔄 MAY / MIGHT

  • may - ehtimol (It may rain)
  • might - kam ehtimol (I might come late)
  • may - ruxsat so'rash (May I come in?)

📝 Testlar

1. I _____ swim when I was five.

2. You _____ eat in the library. (taqiq)

3. _____ you help me?

4. You _____ see a doctor. (maslahat)

5. It _____ rain later, but I'm not sure.

📖 In / On / At

📍 IN

  • in Tashkent, in a room
  • in January, in summer
  • in the morning

📍 ON

  • on the table
  • on Monday
  • on my birthday

📍 AT

  • at the bus stop
  • at 5 PM
  • at night

📝 Testlar

1. I wake up _____ 7 AM.

2. Let's meet _____ Monday.

3. She was born _____ 1995.

4. The book is _____ the table.

5. I drink coffee _____ the morning.

📝 Artikllar

🔵 A

Undosh tovushdan oldin

  • a book, a car, a house

🟢 AN

Unli tovushdan oldin

  • an apple, an egg, an hour

🟡 THE

Aniq, ma'lum narsa

  • the sun, the best

📝 Testlar

1. I have _____ apple.

2. She is _____ engineer.

3. _____ sun rises in the east.

4. He has _____ car.

5. She is _____ best student.

📊 Sifat darajalari

1️⃣ Positive

Oddiy daraja

  • fast, big, beautiful

2️⃣ Comparative

Qiyosiy (ikki narsa)

  • faster than, bigger than

3️⃣ Superlative

Eng yuqori daraja

  • the fastest, the biggest

📝 Testlar

1. This car is _____ (fast) than that one.

2. She is _____ (beautiful) girl in the class.

3. My house is _____ (big) than yours.

4. Today is _____ (hot) day of the year.

5. This is _____ (interesting) book I've read.

⚠️ Noto'g'ri sifatlar

good → better → the best
bad → worse → the worst
far → further/farther → the furthest
little → less → the least
many/much → more → the most

📝 Testlar

1. This is _____ (good) restaurant in town.

2. His health is getting _____ (bad).

3. I have _____ (little) money than you.

4. She has _____ (much) experience than me.

5. This is _____ (bad) day of my life.

🎭 Active & Passive Voice

🎯 Active

Subject + Verb + Object

  • I write a letter.

🔄 Passive

Object + be + V3 + by Subject

  • A letter is written by me.

📝 Testlar

1. I write a book. → A book _____ by me.

2. She ate an apple. → An apple _____ by her.

3. They will build a house. → A house _____ built.

4. He helps me. → I _____ helped by him.

5. She loved him. → He _____ loved by her.

🗣️ Reported Speech

📌 O'zgarishlar

  • Present Simple → Past Simple
  • Present Continuous → Past Continuous
  • will → would
  • can → could
  • now → then

📌 Misollar

  • "I work" → He said he worked
  • "I am reading" → She said she was reading
  • "I will come" → He said he would come

📝 Testlar

1. "I work every day" → He said he _____ every day.

2. "I am reading" → She said she _____ reading.

3. "I will help you" → She said she _____ help me.

4. "I can drive" → He said he _____ drive.

5. "I may be late" → She said she _____ be late.

❓ Question Tags

📌 Qoida

  • Ijobiy gap → inkor tag
  • Inkor gap → ijobiy tag
  • You are a student, aren't you?
  • She doesn't like it, does she?

📝 Testlar

1. You are a student, _____ ?

2. She likes coffee, _____ ?

3. They don't live here, _____ ?

4. He can swim, _____ ?

5. Let's go, _____ ?

🔀 Shart gaplari

Type 0

If + Present Simple, Present Simple

If you heat ice, it melts.

Type 1

If + Present Simple, will + V1

If it rains, I will stay home.

Type 2

If + Past Simple, would + V1

If I were rich, I would travel.

Type 3

If + Past Perfect, would have + V3

If I had known, I would have come.

📝 Testlar

1. If you heat ice, it _____ (melt).

2. If it rains tomorrow, we _____ (stay) home.

3. If I _____ (be) you, I would go.

4. If she _____ (study), she would have passed.

5. If I had known, I _____ (come).
